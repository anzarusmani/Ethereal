Inline Canoeing

Embark on an exhilarating inline canoeing adventure, where you combine the excitement of inline skating with the thrill of canoeing. Glide along picturesque waterways, maneuvering your specially designed inline canoe to navigate twists and turns.

This thrilling water sport offers a unique and unforgettable experience for outdoor enthusiasts and adrenaline seekers alike.

Underground Museum

Delve into the depths of The Subterranean Vault, an extraordinary underground museum that reveals hidden treasures and captivating artifacts. Descend into an underground labyrinth of carefully curated exhibits, showcasing centuries of history and cultural heritage.

Immerse yourself in the mysterious ambiance of this subterranean wonder, where ancient stories come to life through art, artifacts, and interactive displays.

Price: $50 per person

Cliff Rock Resort

Enjoy the serenade of gondoliers as you relax in elegantly decorated rooms, reminiscent of Venetian architecture. Immerse yourself in the allure of Venice's romantic ambiance, making your stay a truly unforgettable experience.

The Cruze

Experience the artistic splendor of Venice at The Cruze Hotel. Inspired by the renowned glass-making island of Murano, this luxurious hotel on sea exudes elegance and sophistication.

Adorned with exquisite glasswork, each room is a testament to Venetian craftsmanship. Indulge in world-class amenities and immerse yourself in the refined atmosphere of this Venetian-inspired haven.

Price: Starting from $500 per night
